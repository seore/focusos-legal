FOCUS OS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Focus OS is a focus and productivity app. We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service and never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data we collect

Account data: your email address and encrypted password, stored securely via Supabase authentication.

Focus session data: session type, duration, and completion time. Used to calculate your focus score and show your history.

App preferences: your peak work hours, notification settings, and onboarding choices.

Purchase data: subscription status managed by RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details.

How we use your data

To provide core app features: focus timer, streak tracking, session history, and weekly stats.

To generate AI insights: your anonymised session patterns are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate personalised recommendations. No personally identifiable information is included.

To send notifications: if you opt in, we send daily focus reminders and streak alerts via push notification.

Data storage

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security — only you can access your own data. Data is stored on servers in the European Union.

Third-party services

Supabase — authentication and database. supabase.com/privacy

Anthropic — AI insights generation (Pro users only). anthropic.com/privacy

RevenueCat — subscription management. revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple — in-app purchases processed via the App Store.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

You can request a copy of your data or ask questions by emailing privacy@focusos.app.

Children

Focus OS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated in-app.
