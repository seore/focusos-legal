Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?

New subscribers get 7 days free. You won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime in your iPhone's App Store settings before the trial ends to avoid charges.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Focus OS → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period.

Why isn't my focus score updating?

Make sure you complete a session fully — the timer must reach zero or you manually end it. Scores update immediately after each completed session.

How do AI insights work?

AI insights are a Pro feature. Once you have enough session data, Focus OS sends anonymised session statistics to Claude AI to generate personalised recommendations. No personal information is shared.

I lost my streak — can it be restored?

Streaks are calculated automatically based on daily sessions. If you believe there's been an error, email us and we'll look into it.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone.

I purchased Pro but it's not activating

Go to Profile → Upgrade to Pro → Restore Purchases. If the issue persists, email us with your Apple ID and we'll resolve it.
